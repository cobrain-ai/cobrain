## Why a CLA?

CoBrain uses a dual-licensing model (AGPL-3.0 + Commercial). To offer commercial licenses, we need to ensure we have the necessary rights to all contributions. This CLA grants CoBrain the rights to:

1. Distribute your contributions under AGPL-3.0
2. Offer commercial licenses that include your contributions
3. Re-license if needed in the future

## How to Sign

### For Individual Contributors
By submitting a PR, you implicitly agree to this CLA. For explicit agreement:
1. Add your name to CONTRIBUTORS.md
2. Include "I agree to the CLA" in your PR description

### For Corporate Contributors
If contributing on behalf of your employer:
1. Email license@cobrain.ai with:
